Culture Clash

The Cheeky Fats

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Oct 29, 2026

Sardinia is a paradise of culinary delights. Beautiful beaches, amazing cheese, and fresh produce. In Italian, "Fresche Fette" translates harmlessly to "Fresh Slices". It sounds delightful. It sounds healthy.

Unfortunately, to the German ear, it sounds absolutely insulting. If you read it out loud, it sounds exactly like "Freche Fette" – which translates to "Cheeky Fat Ones".

Body-Shamed by a Snack

You are on vacation, wearing a swimsuit all day, and now even your groceries are mocking your diet choices. The food might be delicious, but the emotional damage is permanent.
