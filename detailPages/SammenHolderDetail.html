Culture Clash

The Inappropriate Trash Can

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Nov 04, 2026

Norway is a beautiful, peaceful country. And the phrase "sammen holder" is actually a lovely sentiment about holding together or uniting. But if you are an English-speaking tourist walking past this public trash can, your brain is going to read it very, very differently.

Read out loud, this innocent recycling bin suddenly sounds like a highly inappropriate biological storage unit. It is the ultimate culture clash: a wholesome Scandinavian message instantly ruined by the English language.

Context is Everything

We are just glad it is a trash can and not a drinking fountain. Sometimes, it is better not to translate things in your head.
