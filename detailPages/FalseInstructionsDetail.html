Lost in Translation

A Polite Request to Crash

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Oct 27, 2026

Navigating a parking garage in a foreign country is stressful enough. But driving through this specific garage in Vietnam adds an entirely new layer of existential dread. You are just trying to find a spot, and the sign above you offers a very specific, highly dangerous instruction.

Most signs say "Please slow down" or "Drive carefully". This one? It skips the pleasantries. It practically begs you to step on the gas and steer directly into the nearest concrete pillar.

We Blame Google Translate

Somewhere during the translation process, a safety warning turned into a kamikaze mission. We appreciate the politeness of the "Please", but we will respectfully decline the invitation to total our rental car today.
