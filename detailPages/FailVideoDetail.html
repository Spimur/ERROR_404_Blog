The Norman Door

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Nov 07, 2026

We have all been there. You walk up to a glass door. It has a massive handle. Naturally, you grab it and pull. Nothing happens. You pull harder. Still nothing. Then you look down and see a tiny sign that says: PUSH.

You feel stupid, but it is not your fault. It is the fault of the designer. These terribly designed portals of embarrassment are called "Norman Doors", named after Don Norman, the author of "The Design of Everyday Things".

Video Evidence: Why Doors Keep Tricking You

To fully understand this real-life design crime, we highly recommend this mini-documentary by Vox. It proves that a door is only as smart as the person who designed its handle.
