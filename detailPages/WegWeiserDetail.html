Lost in Translation

Drinks at the Stick

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Nov 06, 2026

You are on vacation in Italy, specifically the beautiful island of Sardinia. You want to relax, maybe grab a coffee, an Aperol Spritz, or a cold beer. You look at the multilingual sign to find the nearest "Bar". But if you are German, the sign sends you on a very different quest.

Instead of leaving the word as "Bar", the translator app took it literally and translated it to the German word "Stab" (which means a rod, a pole, or a stick). So the sign is pointing you directly towards "The Stick".

A Very Rigid Happy Hour

"Treffen wir uns im Stab?" (Shall we meet in the stick?) doesn't quite have the same relaxing vacation ring to it. But hey, at least they tried to be helpful!
