Identity Crisis

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Oct 30, 2026

Selling shirts with national flags is a classic tourist trap. It is basically free money. All the designer has to do is google the flag, copy it, and paste it onto a shirt. Apparently, even that was too much work for this clothing store in Thailand.

Take a look at the flag in the middle. Is it Germany? Is it Belgium? If it is Germany, the colors are in the wrong order. If it is Belgium, the flag fell over and is currently taking a nap.

A New Nation Is Born

We are witnessing the birth of a completely new country. Let's call it "Belmany". The capital is Berlin, but they only eat waffles. We would definitely buy that shirt.
