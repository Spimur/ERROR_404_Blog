Lost in Translation

Cannibalism on the Menu

Investigated by Timo Weiler
Oct 25, 2026

Translating a restaurant menu is a high-stakes game. One small typo and your innocent side dish suddenly turns into an international diplomatic incident. Welcome to Vietnam, where alongside your burger, you can order a crispy side of "French Friends".

We really just wanted some fried potato strips, but it seems the chef is offering the highly illegal culinary experience of consuming citizens of France. Do they come with a beret? Are they served with a baguette?

A Tragic Typo

This is a beautiful reminder to double-check your auto-correct before printing 500 leather-bound menus. We politely declined the order. We prefer our side dishes without a passport.
